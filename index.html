About Us

Rural Informative & Social Harmony Academy

Our mission is to provide accessible and tailored education to children living with special needs. We work closely with teachers, families, and village communities to create a long-term positive impact.

  • Individualized learning plans
  • Vocational and livelihood training
  • Parent and community awareness programs
  • Regular health and development camps

Who We Are

We are a community-driven NGO dedicated to supporting children, women, and persons with disabilities through compassion and action.

What We Do
